E-Waste

Management

Application

Industrial revolution threat started during middle of 18th century and continued to flourish throughout 19th and 20th centuries has brought a lot of changes in the way we live. Industrial productiona has moved away from methods of producing by hand towards more sophisticated and automated methods. Research and development in every fact of the industrialization gained momentum and many products were introduced into the market. With the advent of the electrical and alectronic technology, new set of products have hit the markets. Alongside of these developments came the disadvantages and challenges. Rampant production and usage of electronic components have built up huge piles of debris which is termed as 'electronic waste' or 'e-waste'. several government agencies, voluntary organisations, individual scientists and subject matter experts have come together and brainstormed about devising safe methods of disposal of e-waste. The collective efforts of them have shaped up as 'e-waste' Management'.
